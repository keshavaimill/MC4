## üí° Tips for Better Queries

1. **Be specific**: Include mill IDs (M1, M2, M3), recipe IDs (R1‚ÄìR5), or SKU IDs (SKU001‚ÄìSKU014)
2. **Use date ranges**: "next 3 months", "across 2025", "January to June 2026", "last 1 year"
3. **Ask for comparisons**: "compare", "show me all", "which has the highest"
4. **Request charts**: "plot", "show a chart", "show a trend chart", "bar chart of"
5. **Request aggregations**: "total", "sum", "average", "by mill", "by recipe"
